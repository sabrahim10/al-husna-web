Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

Al-Husna is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything the app needs to function is stored locally on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics, crash reporting services, or any third-party tracking tools.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to any server:

• Favorites — names you have saved as favorites

• Notification preferences — your chosen notification frequency and timing

• Widget theme — your selected widget color theme

• Appearance settings — your preferred display mode

• Onboarding state — whether you have completed the onboarding flow

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, Al-Husna will send local push notifications to your device. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

Home Screen Widgets

The Al-Husna widget displays the Name of the Day on your home screen or lock screen. Widget data is computed locally on your device using the Hijri calendar. No data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

Al-Husna does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact
